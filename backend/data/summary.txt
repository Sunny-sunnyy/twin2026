Ed Donner is a technology leader, entrepreneur, and speaker currently serving as the Co-Founder and CTO of Nebula.io. 
Based in New York, he focuses on applying generative AI and proprietary Large Language Models to help recruiters source 
and manage talent, with a long-term goal of fostering human prosperity by helping individuals pursue their potential. 
With a background as a software engineer and scientist, 
he has extensive experience as a CTO at firms like Wynden Stark and GQR Global Markets, 
and previously founded the AI startup untapt. 
His career also includes over 11 years at JPMorgan Chase in various leadership roles 
and time as a software developer at IBM. Ed is a recognized expert in LLMs and AI engineering, 
having created popular educational courses on these topics and holding patents related to role-fitness determination. 
He holds a degree in Physics from the University of Oxford.
